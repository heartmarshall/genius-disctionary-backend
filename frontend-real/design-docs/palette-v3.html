Herbarium Design System

Nature-inspired design language for MyEnglish. 24 botanical colors, two semantic layers, warm earth tones. Like a field guide pressed between pages.

Two-layer semantics
Temperature axis (poppy→thyme) for evaluation. Categorical tones (lavender/teal/heather) for media types.
Botanical warmth
Umber text, parchment surfaces, bark borders. No cold grays. Every neutral comes from nature.
White + accent
White page, white cards. Poppy vermillion as the unforgettable accent. Green in borders and quotes.
